What an experience to record caves! Yordas Cave, Dowkerbottom Cave, Victoria Cave and Ingleborough Cave all in the Yorkshire Dales. Today we measured in total 7 source positions in Dowkerbottom Cave. Sincere thanks to caving expert Chris Jackson for providing access and insights about caving, the area, the types of rock, keeping us safe..
