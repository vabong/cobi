## Venice a Thousand Echoes – Chiesa di Santo Stefano  
*7 April 2024*

[![Chiesa di Santo Stefano](/images/blog/venice-a-thousand-echoes-chiesa-di-santo-stefano/image-1.jpg)](https://www.acousticatlas.de/locations/san_stefano)

As part of the *Listening to Venice* project, acoustic recordings were made at **15:20 on 8 May 2023**, using **red balloon pops** and a **Sennheiser AMBEO VR microphone** to capture the spatial characteristics of the church.
